Weekly Rides

The club has rides every Wednesday and Saturday, leaving from the Portland Building steps, University of Nottingham, at 2pm and 12pm respectively.

Trips

For those that have missed the trips so far, don't panic! The committee have been busy organising various trips for the coming year. Remember that if you are a member, it's your club too, and if you have any suggestions for trips or events, then get in touch with us via e-mail, phone or at the Portland Building before a ride.

Club racing kit

James L
28 October 2005

We will be making an order for new kit soon.  The range of items and prices of each item are shown below;

Short Sleeve Jersey - 30   (or 31 with 3/4 or full length zip)

Polyester Training Top - 33

Gillet - 29

Windtex Jacket - 43

Lycra Bibshorts - 34

Coolmax Bibshorts - 39

Feildsensor Bibshorts - 44

Thermal 3/4 Bibnicks - 35

Thermal Biblongs - 36

Downhill Jersey - 32

Basically you can place an order for any item you want in the TAL brochure;

http://www.endurasport.com/tal-2004.pdf

With the exceptions of smaller items such as armwarmers and overshoes etc.  So if there is anything you would like that is not in the list above just look in the brochure for the price.

All club kit is blue, with black sleeves with nottingham university written accross the chest and back.  Shorts are blue and have a black panel on the side with Nottingham University written on it.  I'm sure you have all seen people around wearing it.

The deadline for the orders will be 4/11/05

If you could email your orders to me at eayyjll@nottingham.ac.uk.  Stating clearly;

1.  Your Name

2.  The item(s) you want

3.  The size of each item (S/M/L/XL)

Could you also place a cheque made payable to U of NSU cycling and place it in the cycling pigeon hole in the AU office in the portland building, with a note saying who it is from and what you have ordered.  NO MONEY NO KIT! 

This is your only chance this year to order any kit, no order will be placed later in the year so get your orders in now!

Thanks

James

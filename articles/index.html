Annual General Meeting

19 March 2002

AGM this WEDNESDAY, 20 MARCH, 7PM Portland Building, Room C20 or C21 (my memory fails me). Apologies for the short notice. Please come along. Social effort afterwards in the Ark. This is your chance to vote for people for the committee, stand for positions on the NUCC committee to bolster that scant CV of yours, and generally try and drive the club in the direction you want it to go. Want more races/less races? More socials? More trips? More training? Less training? Less pestering from Club sec? More emphasis on any area, be it Road, Trials, BMX, DH, XC, active recruitment of totty etc. End of rant.
